Dual Speed Controller

What is it?

Controller for handling two speed controllers connected to the same mechanism. One or both of the motors may be optionally inverted.

Properties

Motor1
The motor to use as the first motor.
Motor1 Inverted
Whether or not the first motor is mounted backwards.
Motor2
The motor to use as the second motor.
Motor2 Inverted
Whether or not the right motor is mounted backwards.
