Sharp IR

What is it?

The Sharp distance sensors are a popular choice for many projects that require accurate distance measurements. This IR sensor is more economical than sonar rangefinders, yet it provides much better performance than other IR alternatives. Interfacing to the RoboRIO is straightforward: the single analog output can be connected to an Analog Input for taking distance measurements.

Two different models of Sharp IR sensors are supported. The GP2Y0A41SK0F senses in the range of 4-30cm, while the GP2Y0A51SK0F senses in the range of 2-15cm.
